Animal with Description

LION

Lions are big cats that are known for their manes and are apex predators. They are native to Africa and India, and are known as the "king of beasts".

ZEBRA

Zebras are social animals with distinctive stripes that live in herds in Africa.

HIPPOPOTAMUS

Hippos are large, semi-aquatic mammals with barrel-shaped bodies, short legs, and big heads. They are native to Africa and are the third largest land mammal after elephants and white rhinoceroses.

KANGAROO

Kangaroos are marsupials with large hind legs, long tails, and pouches for carrying their young. They are native to Australia and are known for their impressive jumping abilities.
